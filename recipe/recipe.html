Homemade Turkey Sandwich

My Favorite Turkey Sandwich: To make a delicious turkey sandwich you need the following list of items as well as a cold bottle of coke to wash it down!

  • bread
  • fresh tomatoes
  • lettuce
  • mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  1. Slice bread in half
  2. Toast bread for thirty seconds
  3. Place a chunk of turkey on bread
  4. Add lettuce and tomatoes
  5. Add a dash of mayonnaise and mustard
  6. Place other slice of bread on top...Enjoy!
